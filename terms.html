My HTML Terminology

My HTML Tags

  • <a>: Hyperlinks
  • <p>: Paragraphs
  • <ul>: Unordered list (bullet points)
  • <ol>: Ordered list (numbers: 1, 2, 3...)
  • <li>: Used within ordered and unordered lists, to establish this text is a part of that list.

  • <head>: The head of a webpage: Contains metadata, or data about data. (i.e. logo, authorship info)
  • <body>: The body of the webpage where most primary information is inserted.
  • <footer>: The footer of a webpage that may contain copyright info, contact info, or related documents.
